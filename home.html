About VRT

The Vanguard Racing Team, an integral component of Pillai College of Engineering situated in New Panvel, Navi-Mumbai, comprises passionate undergraduate engineering students hailing from diverse fields. Eager to foster their growth, development, and enhance their knowledge and soft skills, the team collectively engages in the meticulous design, construction, and manufacturing of an All-Terrain Vehicle (ATV). These dedicated students channel their enthusiasm into participating in esteemed competitions, including Baja SAE, Mega-ATV, and Enduro Student India, showcasing their ingenuity and technical progress on a competitive platform. This collaborative endeavor not only cultivates technical expertise but also fosters a spirit of camaraderie and excellence among the aspiring engineers.

Baja SAE

The Society of Automotive Engineers is in charge of organising the worldwide Baja SAE competition. We mostly participate in Baja SAE India, a competition in which teams from different universities and institutions from other nations compete and showcase their skills, expertise, and creativity through their innovative vehicles (ATV). The major objective of the competition is to design and build an ATV in accordance with their standards and criteria while competing against other universities from July to March each and every year.
